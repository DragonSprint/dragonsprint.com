A full week of sprinting on Pyramid

5 to 9 December 2016, set in the beautiful Ljubljana, Slovenia

Welcome to DragonSprint, a week-long sprint on Pyramid. The sprint takes place in Ljubljana, Slovenia, EU in the first week of December (5th to 9th). The main two sprint topics are Pyramid 2.0 and Pyramid for Newcomers.

The sprint is free to attend for everyone! Contact us if you need help with covering your travel expenses.

Use the menu at the top to read more about the event.

You can join/register for the sprint by:

  • filling out the Signup form,
  • OR joining the dragonsprint@googlegroups.com mailing list and saying Hi!,
  • OR creating your own "sprinter page" by submitting a Pull Request.
